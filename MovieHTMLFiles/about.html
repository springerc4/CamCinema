About CamCinema


About The Creator

My name is Cameron Springer and I am the author behind CamCinema. Since the COVID-19 pandemic began towards the beginning of 2020, I have dedicated much of my free time to watching and analyzing film. My interest in film is still a newfound passion and by no means am I a professional film critic. I do, however, love exploring new movies that convey all sorts of emotions, messages, and artistic visions from those involved in the making of a movie.

Purpose of CamCinema

It has been almost a year since I began consistently watching film and I have seen hundreds of movies over this time. I have made CamCinema in order to reflect on what I have seen and share my personal favorites with an audience. I have broken up these movies into six distinct categories based on the primary genre of the films mentioned. The feedback section of the site also gives you a chance to share your favorite movies with me or share your opinion about any of the movies I have selected.
